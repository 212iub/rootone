Contrast - Atavist Magazine
  • Contrast describes the visual difference between objects close in association.
    Using ocular cues guided by contrast, a designer can direct the viewer's attention
    to specific areas of a webpage to express importance. Contrast is produced
    through the juxtaposition of design elements like color, font, size, and texture/pattern.
